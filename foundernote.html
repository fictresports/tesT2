Our Purpose Founder Note

Founder’s Note

Majid Hameed
Founder & CEO, Fictrpreneur

I didn’t start with capital, investors, or connections. I started with belief and consistency — and that’s what Fictrpreneur is built on. A belief that you don’t need to be born privileged to create something meaningful.

Every person with a dream deserves a fair start. Not after validation — but before it. Because that’s where real entrepreneurship begins.

Why I Started Fictrpreneur

I built Fictrpreneur for those who, like me, had ambition but no map. The world doesn’t lack dreamers — it lacks systems that guide those dreams into execution. Our purpose is to be that system.

What We Stand For

Fictrpreneur is not a startup factory — it’s a launchpad for underdog founders. We exist to help you start small, grow smart, and stay consistent. Every project we support isn’t just an idea — it’s a signal that belief still wins.

The Promise

We’ll continue building the foundation that every first-time founder deserves — mentorship, systems, capital, and a community that lifts each other up. This is more than a company. It’s a movement.

The Future

The next era of entrepreneurship will not come from boardrooms. It will come from bedrooms, hostels, co-working corners, and small-town cafes. And we’ll be there — guiding every step from zero to one.

Because dreams don’t need permission. They just need a place to start.
